Fried Yucca with pork rinds

Yucca is a famous tuber native to Central and South America, it is widely consumed in El Salvador

Its preparation is put to cook the cassava with a little salt until it is soft. Chirmol is prepared by dicing and mixing the tomatoes, onion, green chili and hot chili, salt, pepper and vinegar or lemon.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium yucca
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 garlic
  • Fried pepescas (traditional little fishes) or pork rinds (to taste)
  • Â¼ cabbage
  • 1 carrot
  • tablespoon of oil

Steps:

  1. Cut the yucca into large pieces and boil them in water with salt and garlic
  2. Remove from heat when soft, then cut into smaller portions and fry.
  3. Just like the pepescas, fry them over low heat, so that they cook well.
  4. Grate cabbage, carrot, and onion, place in boiling water and let
    stand for a few minutes, drain the liquid and add vinegar, salt and oregano
  5. Serve the yucca hot along with pepescas and pickles

You can also prepare this cassava dish with pork rinds. For this, just substitute the pepesca for the pork rinds and follow the same instructions.
